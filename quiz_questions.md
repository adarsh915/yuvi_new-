# Original Quiz Questions

Here are the questions that were originally in the hardcoded quiz. You can add these back through the admin panel at `/admin/quiz/questions`.

### Female Questions (Gender: Female)
1. Have you had unprotected intercourse for more than a year without becoming pregnant?
2. Are you nearing or over 35, and have had unprotected intercourse for 6 months without becoming pregnant?
3. Do you have a history of pelvic infection or a sexually transmitted disease?
4. Have you been treated with Clomid without becoming pregnant?
5. Are you 38 years or older and contemplating a pregnancy in the future?
6. Do you have a personal or family history of endometriosis and would like to get pregnant some day?
7. Have you been diagnosed with a uterine abnormality or structural defect?
8. Have you had two or more miscarriages or chemical pregnancies?
9. Do you have a diagnosis of PCOS, PCOD, or insulin resistance?
10. Are your menstrual cycles irregular (longer than 35 days from start to start)?
11. Have you been recently diagnosed with cancer, or have a family history of cancer, and would like to maintain reproductive potential?
12. Do you have a family history of early menopause (occurring before age 45)?
13. Have you been diagnosed with uterine fibroids and would like to conceive in the future?
14. Do you have especially heavy or painful menstrual periods?
15. Do you have unexplained extra hair growth, acne, or breast discharge?
16. Have you been told you have "bad eggs" and would still like to have a baby?
17. Has anyone told you that you are menopausal, but you would still like to conceive?
18. Do you or your husband have a family history of an inherited birth defect, and you would like a child without this problem?
19. Are you or your partner carriers of genetic mutations (Cystic Fibrosis, Sickle Cell, Thalassemia, Fragile X, etc.) you don't want to pass on?

### Male Questions (Gender: Male)
20. Has your male partner had any type of testicular trauma, defect, or surgery?
21. Has your male partner had problems with ejaculation, general infections, or erections?

---

**Note:** When adding these to the admin panel, make sure to set the correct "Order" and "Gender" for each question.
